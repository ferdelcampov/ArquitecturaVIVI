Concept

Compact footprint, generous communal spaces

This five-story mid-rise apartment building organizes 43 units — 32 studios, 6 one-bedroom, and 5 two-bedroom — around a central courtyard planted with palms, opening the ground-floor lobby, pool, and gym directly onto this shared green space. Circulation runs along an open-air corridor that threads past the courtyard on every level, keeping residents in constant contact with light, air, and landscape as they move between units. The ground floor also holds retail, a study room, and package/mail room, while 43 parking spaces sit tucked beneath the building's covered garage. Balconies wrap each floor, extending the building's connection to its surroundings unit by unit, for a total enclosed area of 15,658 SF per floor.
